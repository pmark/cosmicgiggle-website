Architects of the Future

2006 cleanup plan

 

Pre arrival:

  • Minimize extra packaging
  • Avoid boas, glitter, things that blow off and get lost
  • Appoint cleanup team

 

Upon arrival:

  • Educate new members in “Leave No Trace” ethics
  • Provide proper garbage cans so nothing need hit the ground

 

During the week:

  • Don’t throw anything on the ground, only in designated trash containers
  • Clean as we go

 

When leaving:

  • We will pack everything out
  • We will have supplies (trash bags etc.) to pack everything out
  • We will have room in our vehicles to take it with us
  • We will completely take down our camp
  • We will appoint a team to walk over our entire camp area, foot by foot, looking for any stray MOOP that we missed or that blew in from outside.
  • We will educate all camp members that they should leave time during takedown to clean up.
  • We will leave time on Monday morning to clean up carefully.
